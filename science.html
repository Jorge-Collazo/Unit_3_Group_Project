What are N-ice Dude's Ice Cubes and how do they work?

Here at N-ice Dude, we make it our aim to always be revolutionizing even the most simple of objects. Our latest business entry, our Ice Cubes, filled with all-natural flavors that will completely change your drink after dropping just a single cube into it. Currently, at N-ice Dude we have a selection of ten exuberant flavors, but don't worry, if a flavor you want isn't available, know that we are constantly in development of new flavors and you will be able to vote on our social media pages for a chance to get your flavor featured!

N-ice Dude's Ice Cubes are simply cooled cubes that are infused with your flavor of choice. With the purpose of not only cooling your drink, but giving it a unexpected burst of flavor you're sure to enjoy. A single cube can change the way you drink just about anything, but if you would desire, you could add as many cubes as you may like.
