Tea

A traditional flavor that's beloved to just about all people everywhere. From being a simple morning beverage to a sweet drink that's consumed in different parts every day, it can be said that tea is drank everywhere. For such a drink not to receive a twist and allow for further convenience, us at N-ice Dude wish to bring our solution to you!


History

Do you hate putting ice in your tea, only to have the ice melt and water down your drink? We assumed so, so that’s why us at N-ice Dude developed the tea ice, so when you put your ice tea in your iced tea, you won’t have to worry about your flavor being diminished if you wait a while to drink it. The sheer convenience of it allowed the tea flavor to quickly rise up the ranks and secure the top spot out of our flavors.
