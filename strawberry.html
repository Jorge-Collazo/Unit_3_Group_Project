Strawberry

The classic, sweet, and refreshing fruit is back to cool your drink for those hot summer days to keep you hydrated. With each sip, bring flavor to your taste buds with N-ice Dude© flavored ice.


History

Strawberry was one of our first flavors and has been having some of the best sales since. When thinking of our flavors to launch with, we looked to soda for inspiration, such as Crush and Fanta. Using natural flavors, strawberry has stayed as one of our favorite flavors and is available at any store where N-ice Dude flavored ice is sold. We've even be told our customers love to eat them or shave them down for snowcones. Regardless of how you choose to enjoy them, we gurantee you will love strawberry.
